Frequently Asked Questions

Planning to visit or ready to call Faith Church home? Here are answers to popular questions.

What can I expect on Sunday?

Services run about 75 minutes and include live worship, prayer, teaching, and engaging environments for kids birth through 5th grade.

Do you offer online giving?

Yes! Secure giving is available online, in our app, or via text. Set up one-time or recurring gifts in minutes.

How do I join a small group?

Visit the Next Steps area after any gathering or explore open groups online. There are options for every age and schedule.

What if I have kids?

Our Kids Team provides safe, joyful classrooms with secure check-in, age-specific lessons, and trained leaders.
