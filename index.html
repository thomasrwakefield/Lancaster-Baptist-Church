Meet Our Pastor

Pastor Paul & Terrie Chappell

Pastor Chappell and his wife, Terrie, arrived in Lancaster in 1986 with a passion to reach the Antelope Valley with the gospel. Today, God is using Lancaster Baptist Church, Lancaster Baptist School, and West Coast Baptist College to equip believers, strengthen families, and send laborers around the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Getting ready for your first visit? Here are answers to the questions we hear most often.

What are your service times?

Join us Sundays at 8:30 AM and 11:00 AM for morning worship, 5:00 PM for evening service, and Wednesdays at 7:00 PM for Bible study and growth groups.

Where do my children go?

Children enjoy secure check-in, nurseries, and dynamic classes tailored for each age group. Our kids team will help you find the right classroom.

How do I get involved in a class?

Visit the welcome center on Sunday or browse Adult Bible Classes online. Our team will guide you to a class that fits your season of life.

Do you livestream services?

Yes. All services are streamed at lancasterbaptist.org/live with on-demand archives.

Visit Our Lancaster Campus

We are located just east of downtown Lancaster near the intersection of Lancaster Boulevard and 40th Street East. Let us know you’re coming, and our welcome team will be ready.
