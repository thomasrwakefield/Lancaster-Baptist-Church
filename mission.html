Our Mission

Lancaster Baptist Church exists to love God, grow together, and serve others. Everything we do flows from the Great Commission—to make disciples of Jesus Christ locally and globally.

Family Ministry

Families are at the heart of our church. From nurseries and children’s classes to Lancaster Baptist School, we partner with parents to raise Christ-centered kids. Weekly programs include Patch Clubs, Master Clubs, Vacation Bible School, and family-focused events.

Lancaster Baptist School (K-12) provides a strong academic and spiritual foundation, while our childcare center serves infants and preschoolers in a safe, nurturing environment.

Visit LBS

Student & College Ministry

Abundant Life Youth meets each week for Bible preaching, small group discipleship, activities, camps, and mission trips. Our desire is to help teens know Christ personally and stand for Him in their generation.

The college and career ministry, Elevate, provides community for young adults through Bible study groups, campus outreach, and leadership development opportunities.

Community & Global Outreach

Every week, hundreds of volunteers serve through bus routes, hospital visits, Spanish and deaf ministries, addiction recovery, and outreach events like Love Works and Open House Sunday.

Lancaster Baptist supports more than 160 missionary families around the world. Faith Promise Missions giving enables church planting, Bible translation, and humanitarian relief efforts worldwide.
