Plan Your Visit

We can’t wait to meet you! Our ushers and welcome team are ready to help you find parking, check your kids into secure classrooms, and guide you to seating.

Service Schedule

  • Sunday Morning: 8:30 AM & 11:00 AM Worship Services
  • Sunday Classes: 9:45 AM Adult Bible Classes & Student Ministries
  • Sunday Evening: 5:00 PM Worship Service
  • Wednesday: 7:00 PM Bible Study, Kids Clubs, and Student Ministry

Need a Ride?

Call our bus ministry office at (661) 946-4663 to see if a bus route serves your neighborhood.

What to Expect

Expect a friendly greeting, Christ-centered music, and practical Bible preaching. Our services last about 75 minutes. Dress is varied, with many choosing traditional Sunday attire. We encourage you to come as you are.

Families can check-in at the Children’s Welcome Center located near the main lobby. Our team uses a secure tag system for kids and communicates with parents as needed during the service.

Stop by the welcome center after the service to meet our pastors, receive a gift, and learn about next steps.

Directions & Parking

Our campus is located at 4020 E Lancaster Blvd, Lancaster, CA 93535. Enter from 40th Street East and follow the parking team’s guidance. Reserved guest parking is available near the main auditorium entrance.

If you have mobility needs, simply let a parking attendant know. Shuttle carts are available from outlying lots, and wheelchair seating is located in the auditorium.
