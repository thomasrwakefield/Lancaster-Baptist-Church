Our Story

Since 1986, Lancaster Baptist Church has been proclaiming the gospel of Jesus Christ, nurturing families in Scripture, and sending laborers around the world. We believe that God’s Word changes lives, and we’re committed to building a local church that reflects His grace.

How It All Began

In July 1986, Paul and Terrie Chappell followed the Lord’s leading to Lancaster, California. A small group of thirteen believers gathered in a storefront building for the first service. From those humble beginnings, God has grown Lancaster Baptist into a thriving church family reaching thousands each week.

Through seasons of prayer, faith, and sacrificial giving, the church has expanded facilities, launched Lancaster Baptist School, and founded West Coast Baptist College to train the next generation of Christian leaders.

Adult Bible Classes

Lancaster Baptist offers more than 40 adult Bible classes that meet on Sunday mornings at 9:45 AM. Each class provides verse-by-verse Bible teaching, meaningful friendships, and opportunities to serve together. Whether you are single, newly married, a parent, or a senior adult, there is a class designed for your stage of life.

Our Leadership

Lancaster Baptist Church is blessed with a dedicated pastoral team and faithful ministry leaders who love the Lord and serve His people. Pastor Chappell provides vision and Bible preaching, while associate pastors oversee outreach, music, discipleship, student ministries, and family care.

We believe leadership is about serving others. Each member of our staff is committed to connecting people to Christ, cultivating spiritual growth, and encouraging biblical community.
