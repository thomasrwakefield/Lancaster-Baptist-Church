Why We Give

Giving is an act of worship. As we honor the Lord with the firstfruits, we acknowledge that everything we have comes from Him. Our generosity allows the gospel to go forth, missionaries to be sent, and ministry to flourish.

We encourage every member to practice faithful stewardship, consistent generosity, and joyful trust in God’s provision.

Scripture

“Every man according as he purposeth in his heart, so let him give; not grudgingly, or of necessity: for God loveth a cheerful giver.” — 2 Corinthians 9:7
